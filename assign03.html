Hello from Meridian, Idaho, I'm Adrian Lane. This is my last semester and truth be told, I'm looking forward to being done! I've been in IT for many years in some capacity or another, but I'd say the bulk of my experience has been in systems administration, operations, and support. For the last few years, it's been largely spent in direct support of application life-cycle management. I'm currently a DevOps Team Lead, but any position with DevOps in the title is ambiguous at best. My areas of focus are build and release automation, cloud architecture and infrastructure automation, monitoring and support with a little SRE sprinkled in, regulatory compliance, process and standard development, and business continuity. I'm one of those jack-of-all-trades, master of none types... not because I never wanted to specialize in anything, but more so because I was always so dang curious about understanding and getting involved with what everyone else was doing in addition to my own responsibilities. I've been hands on keyboard for a long time, my current career goals are to help others grow in their careers, help organizations set goals to reach new heights, and inspire teams to crush them.
